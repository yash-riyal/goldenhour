System Flow

From 112 call to hospital bed.

112 receives the call; location is auto-derived from the network. The nearest 5 ambulances get a 15-second SOS alert. On arrival, the driver photographs the injury — AI returns severity, blood needs, and the right hospital bed type. A bed gets reserved atomically before the ambulance even moves.
